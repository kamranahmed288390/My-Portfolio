About Me

Full Stack Developer!

Kamran Ahmed is a Full Stack Web Developer with hands-on experience in frontend and backend development, specializing in React.js, Node.js, Express.js, MongoDB, and SQL. He has worked at Data Tronex as both an intern and a full-time developer, contributing to responsive web applications, UI/UX integration, and RESTful API development while managing version control with Git/GitHub. A BS Computer Science graduate from the Institute of Business Management, Karachi, Kamran has built projects ranging from a mobile resale estimator using machine learning to an inventory management system and e-commerce solutions with WordPress and PHP. Skilled in modern frameworks, databases, and tools, he combines technical expertise with problem-solving abilities, and has also taken on leadership roles in tech societies, organizing events, workshops, and volunteer teams.
